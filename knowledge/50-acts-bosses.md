# Acts and bosses (placeholder)

Rough defaults until the research pass fills in real numbers:

- **Before Act 1 boss:** 1–2 removes done, at least one AoE option, a way to
  handle a >30 HP single-target burst phase.
- **Before Act 2 boss:** scaling engine online, answers to artifact / buffs,
  enough block density to survive multi-hit turns.
- **Before Act 3 boss:** burst ceiling matters; know which boss you'll face
  and plan around it.

Default heuristic when unsure: err toward removes and block density;
power-level of the current deck usually beats a marginal new card.
